Tales of the Forgotten Realms

Dare to face your fears in these momentous adventures.

This is a project for a course I'm taking. The website isn't finished, so expect more content in the future. I urge you to try everything you see here and email me should you find any bugs. I'm just beginning to understand how any of this web developing works, so feel free to send me any recommendations or critiques

In this website, so far, you'll be able to select an adventure and play a little with a pre-generated character. You won't have much of a choice, but the idea is that as I progress through the course, you will progress through the game. It doesn't have much functionality yet, and so far there isn't really a way to log in or out of your account, but we'll get there eventually.

I've decided to make a game instead of a business website, because I felt it was more engaging for me. I'm not learning web development for the money, but because it's fun! Add to that that I'm a huge nerd for D&D, and voilá! I figured this would be a great way to practice and get my weekly dose of Dungeons and Dragons.

Without further ado... here's my project!
